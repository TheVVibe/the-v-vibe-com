## Celebrate Makar Sankranti with Thoughtful Vaan Ideas!

Makar Sankranti, a vibrant harvest festival celebrated across India, marks the sun's transition into the Capricorn zodiac sign. It's a time for joyous festivities, kite flying, delicious treats, and the beautiful tradition of 'Sankranti Vaan' or 'Haldi Kumkum'. This custom involves married women exchanging gifts, applying turmeric and vermillion, and offering prayers for marital bliss and prosperity.
